Talk

We determine what the client wants, what they need, what they already have, and what should not be built.

Explain what was built and leave the client able to use it, maintain it, or continue without dependency.

About

Built Where Technology, Storytelling, and Field Experience Meet

Walker Road Studios is not one thing. It is the product of a long, strange career that crossed print journalism, disaster-relief fieldwork, graphic design, software development, game design, and technical writing — and eventually found that those things belong together.

The work here is grounded in real situations, built for real people, and designed to be handed over when it's done.

  • Years of writing and print journalism
  • Humanitarian and disaster-relief field experience
  • Software and application development
  • Game design and interactive experience
  • Years of design and web work
  • Technical writing and documentation
  • Storytelling across multiple forms
  • Practical technology guidance
